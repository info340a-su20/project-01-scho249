About Us

We seek to provide a space for the users to make a list of their dreams, hopes, and aspirations that will maximize the experiences in their life. In our website, the users can search, record, and plan their bucket list and get a refreshment in their daily life. The user can build their own list in BUILD YOUR LIST page by submitting forms and strike out achieved items by clicking them. Further, the user can search for more ideas saved in our website in SEARCH page by entering in keywords. The user will be informed with various ideas related to their keywords and details such as location, expected budget, and best season to help them achieve their dream more efficiently.
